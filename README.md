
# Tracking System for Work Orders (OT)
This system provides a robust tracking tool for work orders, where the operational team manages real-time status updates for all stakeholders (customers, sales departments, and management).

## The Funel Approach: Brewing value
As filtering a good cup of coffee in a chemex or v60, we use a "funel" that filters the process deliver added value.

Our system involves three key actors in a funnel scheme to do so, as you do when filtering a good cup of coffee:

* **Directives** (Strategic): Defines the "recipe." They set the mandatory steps, procedures, and estimated timeframes for each job.

* **Operative** (Tactical): The boots on the ground. They monitor the process, marking each step as clear, delayed, or flagging abnormal situations as they happen.

* **Customer** (Added Value): The beneficiary. They can check the status and estimated delivery time of their order using only their email address and ticket number.

## The Problem it Solves
We eliminate the friction and interruptions. 

There is no need for the client to sign in, fill out complex forms, or saturate communication channels. By providing self-service information, we prevent the "every 10 minutes" status check that interrupts your team's workflow.

Information flows vertically through the funnel, ensuring transparency for the client and focus for the operative. 

The system also provide a small side note or memo if something went of, so the directive can address the abnormality and securing a reliable traceability system
